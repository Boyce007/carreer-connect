About Career Connect

Career Connect is an AI-powered job application platform that automatically applies for jobs in Data Science and Cybersecurity based on your resume.

Key Features

  • AI-driven job matching
  • Automated application submission
  • Real-time job tracking
  • Customizable job preferences
  • Resume and cover letter optimization

How It Works

  1. Upload your resume
  2. AI matches jobs based on skills
  3. Automatically applies for relevant jobs
  4. Track applications in the dashboard
